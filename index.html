How Is This Calculated?

  • Spellcasting Modifier: Spellcasting Ability Modifier + Proficiency Bonus
  • Spell Save: Spellcasting Ability Modifier + Proficiency Bonus + 8
  • Spells Known:
    • Clyde: Spellcasting Ability Modifier + Level
    • Karrde: Spellcasting Ability Modifier + (Level/2)
    • Wormwood: Spellcasting Ability Modifier + (Level/2)
    • The rulebook specifies to round down when dividing level.
  • Martial Arts Die and Total Ki Points are determined by the Monk leveling table
  • Ki Save DC: Wisdom Ability Modifier + Proficiency Bonus + 8

If you see a math error or if I have any incorrect information, please let me know!
